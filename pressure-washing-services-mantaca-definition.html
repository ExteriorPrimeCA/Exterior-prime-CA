Soft washing and high-pressure washing are distinct operational methods used depending on surface durability and contamination type.

This service is often used before inspections, repainting, sealing, or restoration work.

5. Key Distinctions

Pressure Washing vs Soft Washing

Pressure washing uses mechanical force; soft washing uses low pressure and chemical treatment.

Pressure Washing vs Power Washing

Power washing includes heated water, while pressure washing typically does not.

Pressure Washing vs Restoration

Restoration includes repairs and replacement; pressure washing is only surface preparation.

6. Misconceptions

  • Higher pressure always equals better cleaning
  • All surfaces can tolerate the same PSI
  • Chemicals are unnecessary in exterior cleaning
  • Pressure washing replaces repair work
